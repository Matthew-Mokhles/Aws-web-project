About Aswan

Discover the timeless beauty and rich heritage of Egypt's southern treasure on the Nile.

Our Mission

The Aswan Newsletter is dedicated to sharing the stories, culture, history, and beauty of Aswan with the world. We bring you weekly insights into life along the Nile — from archaeological discoveries to Nubian traditions, from travel guides to local events.

Whether you are a resident, a traveler, or simply an admirer of ancient Egyptian civilization, our newsletter connects you to the heart of Upper Egypt. We believe Aswan's story deserves to be told — and we're here to tell it.

What We Cover

History & Archaeology

The latest discoveries, research, and stories from Aswan's ancient sites including Abu Simbel, Philae Temple, and the Unfinished Obelisk.

Nubian Culture

Celebrating the vibrant traditions, music, art, and cuisine of the Nubian people — the soul of Aswan.

Travel & Tourism

Expert guides for exploring Aswan's top attractions, hidden gems, best hotels, and unforgettable Nile cruises.

Nature & Wildlife

From the Nile's birdlife to the Saharan landscapes, discover the natural wonders surrounding Aswan.

Local Events

Stay informed about festivals, exhibitions, community gatherings, and cultural celebrations happening in Aswan.

Heritage Stories

Personal narratives and oral histories that keep Aswan's legacy alive for future generations.
